My story in brief

I am an entrepreneur. Over the years I wear many hats which is covered in my career timeline below. My fundamental curiosity are (a) how does the world work ? (b) what is the future of humanity ? (c) how do we make everyone wealthy ? The Dalai Lama famously said that the purpose of life is happiness. Viktor Frankl said that the meaning of life is to give life meaning. In the pursuit of meaning, Frankl recommends three different kinds of experience: through deeds, the experience of values through some kind of medium (beauty through art, love through a relationship, etc.), or suffering. And Michio Kaku said that there is no fixed purpose to life but rather an active, concious strugle to define and create meaning for onself. I am not sure what is the right answer but it seems it will be closer to 42. Which has led me to see the world of knowledge without boundaries and combinatorial innovation as a primary way of altering the world I live in.

Focus areas: Build long lasting institutions that serve society and expand our understanding of who we are.
